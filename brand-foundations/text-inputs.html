Textbox input

Style notes

Default styling for form labels is the standard paragraph style. Bold paragraph styles or lower level heading styles can also be used for labels when appropriate.

Text elements in forms have a bottom margin of 16px instead of the 24px used elsewhere on business.gov.au.
