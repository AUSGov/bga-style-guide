Style notes

Default styling for form labels is the standard paragraph style. Lower level heading styles can also be used for labels when appropriate.

Text elements in forms have a bottom margin of 16px instead of the 24px used elsewhere on business.gov.au.

How to work out your input credits

First work out the lesser of the following two amounts, then divide that amount by three.

  • Expenditure in acquiring or producing credit inputs and energy inputs for which you have claimed the incentive.
  • To the extent that it is reasonably attributable to the production of the relevant output (expenditure).
