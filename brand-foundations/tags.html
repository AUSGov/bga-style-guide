Tag styles

Style rules

Base tags have 4px padding top and bottom and 8px padding on the left and right.

When borders are applied to tags the padding is adjusted to incorporate the width of the border. e.g. applying a 2px border reduces the padding to 2px top and bottom and 6px on the left and right.
