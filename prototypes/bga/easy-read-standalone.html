What is an Australian business number?

An Australian business number is a unique 11 digit number.

Unique means something that is one of a kind.

Your Australian business number identifies your business.

It shows others that your business is a real business.

Use your Australian business number when you make an invoice for someone that owes you money.

An invoice is a document that shows how much money a person or business owes you.
