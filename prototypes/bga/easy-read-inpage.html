What is an Australian business number?

{% include prototypes/easy-read-standalone.html image="'../../assets/easy-read-ABN-bluebkg.png' | relative_url" text="

An Australian business number is a unique 11 digit number.

Unique means something that is one of a kind.

" classes="standalone" %} {% include prototypes/easy-read-standalone.html image="'../../assets/easy-read-ABNtick-bluebkg.png' | relative_url" text="

Your Australian business number identifies your business.

It shows others that your business is a real business.

" classes="standalone" %} {% include prototypes/easy-read-standalone.html image="'../../assets/easy-ready-invoices.jpg' | relative_url" text="

Use your Australian business number when you make an invoice.

An invoice is a document that shows how much money a person or business owes you.
