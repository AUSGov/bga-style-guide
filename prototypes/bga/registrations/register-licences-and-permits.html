Licences and permits either:

  • give your business approval to do an activity, or
  • help protect your business and employees

Types of licences and permits

Licensing and permit requirements vary by state, local laws and by industry.

The licences and permits you’ll need depend on your:

  • business type
  • business activities
  • location
