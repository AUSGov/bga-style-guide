If you sell food in Australia and you’re not sure which country of origin label to use, this tool is for you. It will help you determine if you need a label. This tool will:

  • provide guidance whether your label needs to include a country of origin statement
  • generate a standard mark to include in your label in png or pdf format

Before you start

You may need to know the following:

  • whether or not your food product contains any Australian ingredients 
  • the percentage of Australian ingredients in your food product 
  • the name of the country where your food product was made (last substantially transformed)
  • whether your food product will be sold packed or unpacked 
  • the name of the country where your food product was packed or packaged 
  • a list of your product’s processing activities that occurred overseas 
  • the average percentage of Australian ingredients 
  • the origin of specific ingredients you want to highlight in your label

Terms of use

The tool is a guide only. Exercise your own judgement and care when using the tool. Your response to each question will determine the label the tool recommends. The information provided through the tool should not be regarded as legal advice and you should consider seeking your own legal advice as appropriate.
