Real Estate and Business Agent Licence (Body Corporate) - Western Australia

You will need this licence if your company intends to buy, sell, lease or manage real estate or a business on behalf of a vendor, landlord, purchaser or tenant.

Companies can appoint people to act as their representative provided they:

  • meet all the eligibility requirements for acting as an agent's representative
  • are registered as a representative of the company.

Real Estate and Business Agents and Sales Representatives Code of Conduct - Western Australia

You need to comply with this code if you intend to practice as a real estate agent, business agent or sales representative.

This code outlines the standards for conduct for real estate agents, business agents and sales representatives and covers topics including:

  • general duties
  • employees
  • conflict of interest
  • appointment and payment of fees
  • specific duties of agents and sales representatives.

Registration as a Real Estate or Business Sales Representative - Western Australia

You will need this registration if you are employed by a licensed real estate or business agent and intend to act as that agent's representative.

There are three registration pathways:

  • unrestricted (can complete sales and property management transactions)
  • restricted to sales transactions
  • restricted to property management transactions.
