Overview

You will need this approval if you intend to undertake development in the following redevelopment areas:

  • Central Perth
  • Armadale
  • Midland
  • Scarborough
  • Subiaco.

Development can be defined as anything from large-scale projects to housing additions, commercial signage and events.
