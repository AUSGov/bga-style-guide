Overview

You will require this permit if you intend to display, hand out or distribute:

  • handbills
  • notices
  • pamphlets
  • flyers
  • any other form of advertising or promotional material to persons or premises.

Eligibility

You must supply a certificate of currency for public liability insurance.

Please consult the responsible council for more information regarding eligibility requirements.
