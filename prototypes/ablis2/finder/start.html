What you'll get

Answer a few questions about your business and receive a customised list of everything you need including;

  • Licences - defined as the need to obtain recognition / certification and registration to undertake a certain business activity.
  • Code of practice - a set of rules which details how people in a certain industry should behave. A code of practice can be defined as a result of legislation or by industry regulators and bodies.
  • Regulatory obligations - an obligation defined in law. A business must comply with relevant services.
  • Advisory material - advice and guidance on undertaking a business.

You can also email the list to yourself when you're done.
