Employees

Will you take on employees?

Independent contractors, sub-contractors and volunteers are not considered employees.

If you're not sure whether you're an employee or a contractor, read about the differences of an employee and contractor or speak to a legal professional.

Will you offer fringe benefits to your employees?

Fringe benefits are non-cash benefits that employers offer to employees, such as a work car for private use, low-interest loans, or movie tickets.
