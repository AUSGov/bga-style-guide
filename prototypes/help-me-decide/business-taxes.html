Business taxes

Do you expect to have a business turnover of $75,000 or more in your first year?

Business turnover is your gross business income (not your profit).

Are you running a taxi business, limousine hire business or providing a ride share service?

This includes:

  • drivers providing ride sharing services
  • taxi drivers who own their taxi
  • taxi drivers who lease or rent a taxi.

This doesn’t include car hire or limousine hire services where you don’t provide a driver.

Will you wholesale or produce wine in Australia, including cider, perry, mead, or sake?

This includes grape wine, grape wine products such as vermouth, fruit or vegetable wines, cider, perry, mead, or sake, that has more than 1.15% ethyl alcohol.

Will you use taxable fuels in factories, equipment, machinery or heavy vehicles?

Heavy vehicles are any vehicle with a GVM (gross vehicle mass) greater than 4.5 tonnes.

Will you import or sell luxury cars?

Luxury cars are cars that are worth more than $64,132 (more than $75,526 for fuel-efficient vehicles).
