Business taxes

Do you expect a turnover of $75,000 or more in your first year?

Turnover is your total business income (not your profit).

Will you provide taxi, limousine or rideshare travel for passengers?

This does not include services where you provide a vehicle only and no driver.

Will you wholesale or produce wine?

This includes grape wine, grape wine products such as vermouth, and fruit or vegetable wines with more than 1.15% ethyl alcohol.

Will you use taxable fuels in factories, equipment, machinery or heavy vehicles (more than 4.5 tonnes)?

A fuel is taxable.

Will you import or sell luxury cars?

Luxury cars are cars that are worth more than $64,132 (or more than $75,526 for fuel-efficient vehicles).
