Business name

Will you use your own name as your business name?

You need to register your business name if it’s different to your own name, or a shortened form of your name.

For example, if your name is Joe Aaron Smith, and you want to use it (or a shortened form such as Joe Smith, J Smith, or J. A. Smith) as your business name, you don’t need to register it.
