Business licence and regulation finder

Who this tool is for

This tool is for business owners who want to know which licences and regulations apply to them.

Start now

Time to complete

Around 5 minutes

Start now

What you'll get

Answer a few questions about your business and get a list of:

  • Licences and registrations - approvals and registrations you need for your business activities.
  • Legal obligations - things you must do by law.
  • Codes and standards - rules that businesses in your industry must follow.
  • Guidance - information on running your business.

You can save the list and email it to yourself when you're done.

What this tool doesn't do.

You can't use this tool to directly apply for licences, permits or other registrations.

You'll get information on how to register and links to the relevant government agencies.
