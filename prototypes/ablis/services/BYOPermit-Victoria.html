Overview

You will need this permit if you are a restaurant, club or a party bus operator and intend to allow people to bring their own liquor for consumption on your premises.

There are two types of permit that can be applied for:

  • BYO permit (restaurants and clubs)
  • BYO permit (party buses).

Check if you are eligible

To be eligible for this permit, you must be at least 18 years old.

Please consult the responsible agency for more information regarding eligibility requirements.
