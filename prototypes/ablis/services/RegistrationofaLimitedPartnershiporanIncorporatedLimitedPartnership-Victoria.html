Overview

You will need this registration if you intend to operate as a limited partnership or an incorporated limited partnership.

A partnership is an entity comprised of 2 or more people that jointly carries on business for profit.

  • limited partnership is one where the liability of one or more partners for the debts and obligations of the business is limited. A limited partnership consists of one or more general partners (whose liability is unlimited) and one or more limited partners
  • incorporated limited partnership is a special corporate form of partnership primarily established for people engaged in high-risk venture capital projects.

Check if you are eligible

To be eligible for registration, the limited partnership must have:

  • a registered office in Victoria
  • at least 1 general partner (and a maximum of 20)
  • at least 1 limited partner (but there is no maximum number of limited partners).
