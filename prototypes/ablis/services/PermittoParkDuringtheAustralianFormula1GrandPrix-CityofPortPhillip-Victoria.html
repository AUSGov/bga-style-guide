Overview

You will require this permit if you intend to park a vehicle in a designated area during the race period of the Australian Formula 1 Grand Prix.

Eligibility

This permit applies to the local areas surrounding the race course during the event period. Please refer to the relevant local council for details of any specific requirements for this permit, and to ascertain the areas affected.
