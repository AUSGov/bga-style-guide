Overview

You will need this licence if you operate a business or club that supplies alcohol for consumption on the premises.

The licence allows you to sell alcohol to drink on your licensed premises without also being required to serve meals. Please note, it does not allow you to sell takeaway alcohol.

How long does it last?

12 months or from date granted until end of calendar year

Eligibility

To be eligible for this licence, you must:

  • be at least 18 years old (if you are applying as an individual)
  • consent to a national police check.
