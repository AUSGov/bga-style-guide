Overview

You will need this permit if you intend to operate an outdoor dining facility in on-street car parking bays directly outside your business, or the neighbouring business with permission.

A parklet is a car parking bay repurposed to create space for hospitality businesses to extend their trading.

How long does it last?

Annual permits are from October 1st to September 30th. Seasonal permits are from October 1st to 30th April.

Eligibility

To be eligible for this permit, you must:

  • hold public liability insurance for the amount determined appropriate by Council
  • meet the location and traffic conditions as specified by Council.

Please note, this permit is exclusively for hospitality businesses. Please consult the Council for more information regarding eligibility requirements.
