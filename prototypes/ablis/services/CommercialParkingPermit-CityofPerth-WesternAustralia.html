Overview

You will need this permit if you intend to park in a restricted area or contrary to council parking laws. For example, this permit may be granted for a parking spot on a public thoroughfare or in a public parking facility.

The location and type of parking permit will vary. Please contact the council for more information.

Eligibility

Please contact the council to identify any eligibility requirements.
