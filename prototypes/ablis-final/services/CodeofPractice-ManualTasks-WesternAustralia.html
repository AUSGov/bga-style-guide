Overview

You will need to comply with this code if you intend to undertake manual tasks.

The code of practice provides:

  • general guidance for employers and workers on the identification, assessment and control of safety and health hazards and risk associated with manual tasks and tools that expose workers to vibration.
  • information on key legislative requirements relating to hazardous manual tasks.
