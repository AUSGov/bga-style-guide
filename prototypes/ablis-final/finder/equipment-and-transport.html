What is ‘plant’?

Plant means:

  • machinery
  • equipment
  • appliances
  • containers
  • implements 
  • tools 
  • anything fitted or connected to those things. 

For example:

  • amusement devices.  
  • quad bikes 
  • power tools 
  • vehicles 
  • forklifts 
  • conveyors 
  • machinery 
  • computers 
  • cranes 
  • lifts
