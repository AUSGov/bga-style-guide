Guidelines for creating your country of origin statement

If the country of origin statement will be printed or glued on the packaging, the statement must be in a clearly defined box.

For example:

Grown in <country>

Produced in <country>

Made in <country>

If the country of origin statement will be on display at the point of sale, no defined text box or formatting is required (this only applies to fresh fruit and vegetables in transparent packaging).

For example:

Grown in <country>

Produced in <country>

Made in <country>
