Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The ABLIS related laws component displays relevant ACT and regulation names on ABLIS information pages.

Where possible the ACT or regulation names should link to more information.

Where to use

This component is used on ABLIS information pages. It should be placed last on the page, just above the ABLIS site footer. This component should not be included in the anchor menu.
