Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The global pathway to services provides the user with links to business.gov.au related services.

When to use this component:

  • Used across every page of business.gov.au.

Rules

  • The left side link is used to provide a return link for users that have come directly from a business.gov.au service or subsite (e.g export.business.gov.au). If users have not come from a business.gov.au product no link is displayed.
  • The return link uses the wording 'Return to ...'.
  • The right side button reveals a dropdown list of sign in pages for government business portals.
