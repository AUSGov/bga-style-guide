Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The Contact call out box component is used to display contact details for the Business .gov.au contact centre, or one of its programs. 

When to use this component:

  • On pages that may require customer to contact the department for additional help. Generally these will be the program pages.

Content rules:

  • There are pre-created fields on this component that should be used as per their determination (e.g. add phone number to the phone number field).
