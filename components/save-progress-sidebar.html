Sticky behaviour

Desktop

The save your progress sidebar displays sticky behaviour when the users scrolls down the page. The sidebar is fixed in place when it is 32px from the top of the screen. The sidebar position is unfixed at the bottom of the page so it sits just above the site footer and share utilities.

Mobile

Save your progress sidebar is fixed at the bottom of the screen along with the chat button. The “clear checklist progress” link displays underneath the checklist. It is not fixed in the sidebar.

Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The save your progress sidebar provides the ability to save and email a link recording the users progress though a process.

Clicking on the 'save your progress' button triggers a modal with email form.

When to use this component:

  • Used with the standard checklist.
