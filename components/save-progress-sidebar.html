Sticky behaviour

On screens wider than 992px the save your progress sidebar becomes fixed 32px from the top of the screen when the user scrolls down the screen.

On screens narrower than 992px the save you progress sidebar does not display sticky behaviour. It is instead stacked underneath the page content.

Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The save your progress sidebar provides the ability to save and email a link recording the users progress though a process.

Clicking on the 'save your progress' button triggers a modal with email form.

When to use this component:

  • Used with the standard checklist.
  • In tools such as the lean business plan builder
