Guidelines for use

Purpose:

Used in ALBIS to display a collection of results at the end of a tool process. The two different result tiles are used in different tool contexts:

  • ABLIS action links are used in the 'Apply now' section of ABLIS information pages to present application links.
  • ABLIS key information links are used in the 'Key information' section of the ABLIS information pages to present important resources that are not application links.
