Guidelines for use

Purpose:

The Get contract call to action is used to provide users with the ability to downoad or email a document. The component includes a verification process for verifying the users email address.

When to use this component:

  • Used in the Employment Contract Tool to allow users to download or email an employment contract.
  • This component could be adapted to downloading / emailing documents in other contexts.
